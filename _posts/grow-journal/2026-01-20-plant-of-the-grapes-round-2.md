Day 2: Now that the seed has successfuly sprouted. I transferred the seedling to the felt pot (12 inches diameter, 10 inches tall), and buried it about .5 inches below the surface. Add several drops of water on top of the soil to help grow. I then place a clear, plastic "dome" over the area to help maintainthe humidity level in its early stages.
